Program Details & FAQs

Clear answers to common questions about medical weight loss.

What Type of Diet is Followed?

The diet in a medical weight loss program is customized for the individual. Most people find success with a controlled carbohydrate, reduced-calorie diet. Our medical staff provides the guidance and tools you need to identify and prepare healthy foods that support your weight loss journey.

What Type of Medications Are Used?

For qualified patients, the program may include a prescription appetite suppressant, usually taken in the morning to help curb hunger throughout the day. Some patients also find support from supplements such as Nutrilite Carb Blocker 2 (helps reduce carb absorption) or Nutrilite Slimmetry (a blend of natural herbs). B-12 injections may also be offered to boost energy and support fat burning.
